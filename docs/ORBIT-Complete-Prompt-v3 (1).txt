================================================================================
  PART 1 — THE 27 LAWS OF HUMAN BEHAVIOR
================================================================================

  Har feature design karne se pehle, in 27 laws ke against check karo. Agar
  ek bhi law violate ho raha hai, feature wahin reject karo ya redesign karo.

  ── BEHAVIORAL TRUTHS (Economic) ─────────────────────────────────────────────

  LAW 01 · Users decode every system within 7 days. Agar credit = rupee hai,
          wo pata chal jayega. Therefore: ENCODE credits AS game score, not
          currency. Variable rewards. Hidden exchange rate.

  LAW 02 · Invisible punishment feels like scam. Silent shadow-ban → 1-star
          review on Play Store. Therefore: ALWAYS show symptom, even if not
          the algorithm. "Account Health: Quality Fair" > invisible penalty.

  LAW 03 · Fragmentation = death. 100 rooms × 5 users = ghost town. Therefore:
          CONSOLIDATE ruthlessly. 20 hot rooms > 200 dead rooms.

  LAW 04 · Incentives get gamed. Invite reward = fake account farm. Therefore:
          MULTI-STAGE unlocks. Pay the inviter only when invitee stays.

  LAW 05 · Every role becomes crowded. Everyone wants creator badge. Therefore:
          SCARCITY. Limited slots per category. Rare = valuable.

  LAW 06 · Ranking = optimization target. Simple metric = simple gaming.
          Therefore: MULTI-FACTOR ranking (velocity + diversity + quality +
          retention). Change weights secretly monthly.

  LAW 07 · Earning-first UX = mercenary users. They'll follow money elsewhere.
          Therefore: EMOTIONAL UX first. Friends, story, connection. Earning
          is a side-effect.

  LAW 08 · Day-1 identity is sacred. Empty profile = no attachment = churn.
          Therefore: INSTANT identity. Username + avatar + badge + starter
          credits + starter rooms in <60 seconds.

  LAW 09 · Bored users don't ask — they leave. No exit ramp = silent churn.
          Therefore: BUILD escape hatches. Quiet-time suggestions, pause
          mode, comeback rewards.

  LAW 10 · Your real competitor is Reels, not another social app. Effort
          asymmetry kills. Therefore: LOW-EFFORT loops. Scroll + react =
          micro-earn. Listen mode. Quick reactions.

  LAW 11 · Too-sanitized = dead. No drama = no stickiness. Therefore:
          CONTROLLED CHAOS. Debate arenas, roast battles, chaos days.

  LAW 12 · Founder can't scale moderation. Therefore: COMMUNITY marshals
          + AI layer + escalation ladder.

  ── BEHAVIORAL TRUTHS (Psychological) ─────────────────────────────────────────

  LAW 13 · Loss hurts more than gain pleases (Kahneman). Therefore: PROTECT
          streaks, badges, status. Make losing feel visible, costly.

  LAW 14 · Humans follow tribe, not numbers. "250 online" < "3 friends here".
          Therefore: SOCIAL PROOF via faces, not stats.

  LAW 15 · First 15 minutes decide everything. Onboard to "WOW" moment fast.
          Therefore: AUTO-magic first interaction. Force success.

  LAW 16 · Empty room = embarrassment = churn. Therefore: ZERO-LONELINESS.
          AI companions (clearly labeled), warm-state UI, redirect from dead
          rooms.

  LAW 17 · Status > cash for power users. Therefore: PRESTIGE rewards — gold
          borders, Hall of Fame, physical merch, event invites.

  LAW 18 · Heavy earners burn out and blame app. Therefore: ANTI-FATIGUE
          caps + forced cooldowns + sleep-hour reduced earning.

  LAW 19 · Permaban = 1-star review + Twitter rant. Therefore: GRACEFUL
          demotion. Always a redemption quest.

  LAW 20 · 90% lurk, 9% engage, 1% create. Design for the 90%. Therefore:
          LURKER economy — listen credits, observer badges, shy mode.

  LAW 21 · India is NOT a Silicon Valley clone. Regional, cricket, festival,
          language, UPI, Paytm. Therefore: CULTURAL FIT — local rooms,
          festival auto-spawns, vernacular surfacing.

  LAW 22 · Notification spam = uninstall. Therefore: NOTIFICATION TAX — max
          3/day, AI-curated, emotional not promotional.

  LAW 23 · Indians value privacy. Therefore: PRIVACY-AS-PREMIUM — invisible
          mode, ghost profile, incognito rooms (Pro features).

  LAW 24 · Users must feel progress even when offline. Therefore: OFFLINE-FIRST
          preview — cached credits, pending earnings, local streak countdown.

  LAW 25 · Week needs a shape. Monday blur = low engagement. Therefore:
          WEEKLY RHYTHM — Monday Fresh, Wednesday Chaos, Sunday Wrap.

  LAW 26 · Empty screen = depressed mood. Therefore: ANTI-GHOST UI — rolling
          stats, presence particles, recent-actives-floating.

  LAW 27 · Easy exit = no second chance. Therefore: PAIN-OF-EXIT — show what
          user loses, offer pause not quit, win-back SMS after 7 days.

  PART 5 — CREDIT ECONOMY 2.0
  (Fix for Problem 1: Credit ≠ Rupee Confusion)
================================================================================

  ROOT CAUSE:
    v7 had clean 10 credits = ₹1 mapping. Users will decode this within a
    week. Then every message earning feels like "kitne paise mile" — direct
    rupee comparison → disappointment → churn.

  PSYCHOLOGICAL PRINCIPLE (Skinner, Variable Ratio Reinforcement):
    Fixed rewards → habituation.
    Variable rewards → dopamine addiction.
    Slot machines use this. So do Instagram likes.

  SOLUTION: Decouple perceived value from real value.

  ── 5.1 · CURRENCY REBRAND ────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  ❌ OLD (v7)        →   ✅ NEW (v3)
  "Credits"              "ORBIT Points" (OP) or "Sparks" — user-chosen at onboarding
  "₹" symbol             💎 gem icon (never rupee symbol except cashout screen)
  Exact number           Display-mode with banding:
                           • <100: exact ("💎 87")
                           • 100-999: exact ("💎 234")
                           • 1K-9.9K: rounded ("💎 2.3K")
                           • 10K+: banded ("💎 12K+")
  Daily earning          "Today's vibe: ~230" (range, not exact)
  Rupee mention          NEVER in-app except on /cashout screen

  ── 5.2 · VARIABLE EARNING ENGINE ─────────────────────────────────────────────

  Base message earning (was: flat 5 credits in v7):

    function calculateMessageReward(msg, user, room):
      base = random(2, 15)                         // variable!
      qualityMultiplier = 0.5 to 2.0 (AI-scored)   // caps extremes
      streakMultiplier = streakDays >= 30 ? 2.0 :
                          streakDays >= 7  ? 1.5 :
                          streakDays >= 3  ? 1.2 : 1.0
      roomHeatBonus = room.isHot ? 1.3 : 1.0
      newUserBoost = user.ageInDays < 7 ? 1.5 : 1.0
      dailyCap = applyDailyDimishing(user.messagesToday)
      // 1-50: 100%, 51-100: 80%, 101+: 50%

      raw = base * qualityMultiplier * streakMultiplier
            * roomHeatBonus * newUserBoost * dailyCap

      // Mystery bonus trigger (every 8-12th message randomly)
      if user.messagesToday % random(8,12) == 0:
        raw += random(20, 100)   // dopamine hit

      return Math.round(raw)

  transactions/{tid}:

  1. NEVER send back exact INR in any API response (except /cashout)

  PART 6 — ACCOUNT HEALTH SYSTEM
  (Fix for Problem 2: Invisible Trust Score)
================================================================================

  ROOT CAUSE:
    v7 has Layer-4 Trust Engine that silently reduces earning for spammers.
    But user sees: "Yesterday I got 200, today only 80" → concludes "app is
    fake/scam" → 1-star Play Store review → viral drop.

  PRINCIPLE (Procedural Justice — Tom Tyler):
    People accept unfair outcomes IF the process is transparent.
    "Earning dropped because I spammed" is accepted.
    "Earning dropped for no reason" causes rage.

  SOLUTION: Surface symptoms, hide algorithm, gamify recovery.

  ── 6.1 · THE 5-METRIC HEALTH CARD ────────────────────────────────────────────

  User-facing card in Profile → Account Health:

    ┌────────────────────────────────────────────┐
    │  🫀 ACCOUNT HEALTH                         │
    │  Overall: GREAT  (82/100)                  │
    │                                            │
    │  🎯 Quality       ████████░░  Great         │
    │  🔥 Consistency   ██████░░░░  Good          │
    │  💬 Community     ██████████  Elite         │
    │  ✨ Originality   ███████░░░  Good          │
    │  🏆 Reputation    █████████░  Great         │
    │                                            │
    │  ↗️  Your earning boost: +15%               │
    │  💡 Tip: Try replying to others' messages  │
    │      to raise Community score              │
    └────────────────────────────────────────────┘

  5 Metrics Explained (shown on tap):

    Quality      — Are your messages thoughtful? (length, AI score, reactions)
    Consistency  — Do you show up regularly? (streak + daily active)
    Community    — Do you engage with OTHERS, or monologue? (replies ratio)
    Originality  — Copy-paste detection + uniqueness check
    Reputation   — Reports against you, helpful upvotes, room kicks

  ── 6.2 · THE FIVE LEVELS ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  Each metric 0–100, binned into:
    0–20   : "Poor"   🔴  →  -30% earning
    21–40  : "Fair"   🟠  →  -10% earning
    41–60  : "Good"   🟡  →  base earning
    61–80  : "Great"  🟢  →  +15% earning
    81–100 : "Elite"  💎  →  +25% earning

  ── 6.3 · TRANSPARENT FEEDBACK TRIGGERS ───────────────────────────────────────

  When a metric DROPS by a level:
    • In-app toast: "Your Quality dropped to Fair — messages too short lately"
    • Profile badge: orange alert dot on Account Health tab
    • Push (if drop is >1 level): "Your earning rate decreased by 10%.
      Tap to see how to restore it."

  When a metric RISES:
    • Celebration animation: "Reputation rose to Elite! 💎 +25% earning"
    • Shared to friend feed (optional): "Raj reached Elite Reputation"

  ── 6.4 · REDEMPTION QUESTS ───────────────────────────────────────────────────

  When ANY metric is Poor/Fair, offer a 3-step quest:

    ┌────────────────────────────────────────────┐
    │  ⚡ QUALITY QUEST                          │
    │  Restore your Quality score to Good        │
    │                                            │
    │  ✓ Write 5 messages over 20 chars each    │
    │  ☐ Get 3 positive reactions on 1 message  │
    │  ☐ Help a new user (respond to @welcome)  │
    │                                            │
    │  Reward: Quality +20 points                │
    │  Bonus:  200 💎 on completion              │
    └────────────────────────────────────────────┘

  Quests visible in bottom nav badge, disappear when complete.

  ── 6.5 · FIRESTORE SCHEMA ────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  users/{uid}/health/current:
    quality:      { score: 0-100, level: string, lastChange: ts, deltaReason: string }
    consistency:  { score, level, lastChange, deltaReason }
    community:    { score, level, lastChange, deltaReason }
    originality:  { score, level, lastChange, deltaReason }
    reputation:   { score, level, lastChange, deltaReason }
    overall:      number (weighted average)
    earningBoost: number (-30 to +25, in percent)
    trend:        "rising" | "stable" | "falling"

  users/{uid}/health/history:
    daily:    [{ date, metrics: {...}, overall }]   // 30-day rolling
    weekly:   [{ weekStart, avg }]                  // 12-week rolling

  users/{uid}/quests/{questId}:
    type: "restore-quality" | "boost-community" | ...
    steps: [{ description, required, current, done }]
    reward: { points, healthDelta }
    expiresAt: timestamp
    status: "active" | "completed" | "expired"

  ── 6.6 · CLOUD FUNCTION ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  scheduleHealthRecalc = scheduled("every 2 hours")
    for each active user:
      recalculate 5 metrics from last 48h activity
      diff against previous
      if level changed:
        write to health/history
        trigger in-app toast (write to notifications/)
        if dropped >1 level: queue push notification
        if any metric now Poor/Fair: auto-generate redemption quest

  ── 6.7 · THE TRUST EQUATION (internal, never exposed) ────────────────────────

  earningMultiplier = 0.7 + (overall / 100) * 0.55
    → overall=0   → 0.70x (30% penalty)
    → overall=50  → 0.975x (base)
    → overall=100 → 1.25x (25% boost)

  This is the ONLY place the algorithm touches earnings.
  User sees ONLY the surfaced metrics, never the formula.



================================================================================
  PART 7 — ROOM LIFECYCLE ENGINE
  (Fix for Problem 3: Ghost Town Rooms)
================================================================================

  ROOT CAUSE:
    Unlimited room creation + no archival = 1000 users × 500 rooms = dead app.
    User opens feed, sees 10 rooms, each with "last msg 3 days ago" → uninstall.

  PRINCIPLE (Network Density):
    Social apps need MINIMUM VIABLE ACTIVITY per unit area. Reddit forces
    subscribe. Clubhouse auto-expires rooms. WhatsApp has no "browse".

  SOLUTION: Force consolidation. Hide dead rooms. Surface hot ones.

  ── 7.1 · ROOM TYPES + LIFECYCLE RULES ────────────────────────────────────────

  TYPE            CREATE LIMIT     INACTIVITY → ARCHIVE    RESTORE TRIGGER
  ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Casual Room     1/user/day       24h no message          10 msgs in 1h
  Mood Room       2/user/day       12h no message          5 msgs in 30 min
  Event Room      3/user/week      Auto-end at schedule    Cannot restore
  Permanent Room  Creator+ tier    Never archive           N/A
  City Room       Admin-only       Never archive           N/A (always live)
  Festival Room   Auto-spawn       Ends with festival      Yearly re-spawn

  ── 7.2 · AUTO-ARCHIVE CLOUD FUNCTION ─────────────────────────────────────────

  runEveryHour:
    for each room where status = "active":
      timeSinceLastMessage = now - room.lastMessageAt
      if timeSinceLastMessage > threshold[room.type]:
        room.status = "dormant"           // hidden from discovery
        // kept visible in "My Rooms" for members
      if room.status == "dormant" and timeSinceLastMessage > 7 days:
        room.status = "archived"          // removed from "My Rooms"
        members receive soft notification: "Room 'X' is sleeping"

  runEveryMinute:
    for each room where status = "dormant":
      msgsLastHour = count(messages where timestamp > now-1h)
      if msgsLastHour >= revivalThreshold[room.type]:
        room.status = "active"            // auto-restored
        broadcast: "🔥 Room 'X' is back alive!"

  ── 7.3 · THE HOT 20 ALGORITHM ────────────────────────────────────────────────

  Discovery feed only shows 20 rooms at a time. Ranking:

  hotScore = 0.25 * velocity          // msgs per minute (last 15 min)
           + 0.20 * diversity         // unique users in last hour / total members
           + 0.15 * replyDepth        // avg thread depth
           + 0.15 * qualityAvg        // AI-scored msg quality average
           + 0.15 * retention         // % members returning within 24h
           + 0.10 * recency           // decay: halves every 6h

  Update every 2 minutes (cached in Firestore /hotRooms/current).

  ── 7.4 · FEED STRUCTURE ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  Discovery tab has FIVE sections, top-to-bottom:

  1. 🔥 HOT NOW          — Top 20 hot rooms (global)
  2. 🏙️  NEAR YOU         — City/region-based (Chandigarh, Punjab etc.)
  3. ✨ FRESH TODAY      — Rooms created <24h with any activity
  4. 🎯 FOR YOU          — Personalized (interest-matched, ranked by hotScore)
  5. 🎉 HAPPENING SOON   — Scheduled events starting in <6h

  "My Rooms" is a SEPARATE tab, not in Discovery. Own rooms always visible
  to member even if dormant (with grayed tint).

  ── 7.5 · ROOM CREATION FRICTION ──────────────────────────────────────────────

  Creating a room triggers a 3-step process (prevents spam):
    Step 1: Name + category + vibe tag
    Step 2: Description (min 30 chars) + 3 starter message prompts
    Step 3: "Invite 3 members before going live" (social commitment)

  NEW RULE: If room has <3 messages in first 2 hours → auto-archived.

  ── 7.6 · MERGE SUGGESTIONS ───────────────────────────────────────────────────

  Cloud Function scans similar-themed rooms nightly:
    If two rooms have:
      • >70% category overlap
      • <20 active members each

  PART 8 — ANTI-ABUSE INVITE SYSTEM
  (Fix for Problem 4: Squad System Gaming)
================================================================================

  ROOT CAUSE:
    v2 prompt: "Invite friend, they stay 3 days, you get ₹100."
    India reality: Users will spin up 10 fake accounts via dual-SIM phones,
    virtual numbers, recycled SIMs from scrap dealers (real market, ~₹20/SIM).
    Then "stay 3 days" = just open app 3 times. You're bankrupted in a week.

  PRINCIPLE (Principal-Agent Problem):
    Reward the behavior you ACTUALLY want, not a proxy.
    You want: real engaged users. Not just "signups".
    Therefore: pay ONLY when invitee becomes provably valuable.

  SOLUTION: Multi-stage unlock + device/SIM fingerprinting + activity gates.

  ── 8.1 · THE 4-STAGE INVITE UNLOCK ───────────────────────────────────────────

  Stage 0 (invite sent): Inviter sees "Pending" card, earns NOTHING.

  Stage 1 (invitee signs up):
    Requires: Indian phone OTP verified
    Requires: device fingerprint not seen as invitee before (hard block)
    Requires: SIM operator detection (blocked if virtual-number services)
    REWARD: Inviter +20 💎 ("Welcome bonus")

  Stage 2 (invitee active Day 2-7):
    Requires: 5 messages per day × 5 days (can skip 2 days)
    Requires: Account Health overall ≥ 50 (not a spam bot)
    Requires: Joined at least 2 rooms + posted in each
    REWARD: Inviter +100 💎 (on Day 7 at 00:00 IST)

  Stage 3 (invitee crosses 50 💎 earning of their own):
    Requires: Their own lifetimePoints ≥ 50 (proves genuine activity)
    REWARD: Inviter +200 💎

  Stage 4 (invitee makes first purchase OR first cashout):
    REWARD: Inviter +500 💎 (one-time, rarely triggered — valuable users)

  Total max reward per invitee: 820 💎 ≈ ₹82
  Cost per real user acquired: ₹82 (vs organic CAC ₹150+ → net profit)

  ── 8.2 · DEVICE / SIM FINGERPRINTING ─────────────────────────────────────────

  On signup, capture:
    - Firebase App Check token (Play Integrity / DeviceCheck)
    - Device model + OS + build
    - IP + geolocation country
    - SIM info (via native API): operator, country, MCC+MNC
    - Client-generated deviceId (UUID stored in encrypted keychain)

  BLOCKS:
    ✗ Device previously used for another signup (cooldown: 60 days)
    ✗ SIM from known virtual-number services (Twilio, Plivo India, etc.)
    ✗ IP from datacenter ASN
    ✗ >3 signups in 24h from same IP
    ✗ Emulator detection (Play Integrity flag)

  Store in:
    fraudGuard/signupSignals/{uid}:

      flaggedReasons: [...]
      reviewStatus: "auto-approved" | "pending" | "rejected"

================================================================================
  PART 10 — QUALITY-WEIGHTED DISCOVERY
  (Fix for Problem 6: Discovery Manipulation)
================================================================================

  ROOT CAUSE:
    Single-metric ranking (e.g. messages/hour) → users spam to top.
    Simple gaming → low-quality chaff tops feed → users leave.

  PRINCIPLE (Goodhart's Law):
    "When a measure becomes a target, it ceases to be a good measure."
    Solution: Use 6 measures. Rotate weights. Keep formula opaque.

  SOLUTION: Multi-signal ranking with anti-farm detection + editorial curation.

  ── 10.1 · RANKING SIGNALS ────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  Room hotScore (from Part 7):
    hotScore = 0.25 * velocity
             + 0.20 * diversity
             + 0.15 * replyDepth
             + 0.15 * qualityAvg
             + 0.15 * retention
             + 0.10 * recency_decay

  Weights are REBALANCED monthly by the ORBIT team (anti-gaming).
  Users never see exact weights. Public docs say: "Quality + activity + diversity."

  ── 10.2 · SIGNAL DEFINITIONS ─────────────────────────────────────────────────

  velocity (0-1 normalized):
    msgsLast15Min / rollingAvg15MinPast7Days
    Caps at 3.0, normalized.

  diversity (0-1):
    uniqueUsersLast1h / max(membersTotal, 20)
    Penalizes "same 3 users chat constantly".

  replyDepth (0-1):
    avgThreadLength (in-reply-to chains) / 5.0
    Capped. Encourages conversation, not monologues.

  qualityAvg (0-1):
    Gemini-scored quality of last 50 messages (composite: length,
    toxicity-free, originality, helpfulness).
    Runs on sample, not every message (cost control).

  retention (0-1):
    Members who visited room today / members who visited yesterday.

  recency_decay:
    exp(-hoursElapsed / 6)
    After 6h, score halves.

  ── 10.3 · ANTI-FARM DETECTION ────────────────────────────────────────────────

  Scheduled Function (every 5 min) flags rooms with:

    FARM_FLAG_1: >80% of messages by 3 or fewer users (echo chamber)
    FARM_FLAG_2: Avg message length <8 chars (spam)
    FARM_FLAG_3: Inter-message timing pattern bot-like (std dev <2 sec)
    FARM_FLAG_4: Same user posting >50% of messages
    FARM_FLAG_5: No new users joined in 24h despite high velocity
    FARM_FLAG_6: Meilisearch similarity between messages >85% (copy-paste)

  If 2+ flags triggered:
    - Room's hotScore multiplied by 0.3 (hard penalty)
    - "Under Review" badge shown to non-members
    - Admin notified
    - Marshal team auto-assigned to review

  If 4+ flags triggered:
    - Room temporarily suspended from discovery
    - Creator + members notified with reason
    - Appeal window: 48h

  ── 10.4 · DISCOVERY MIX STRATEGY ─────────────────────────────────────────────

  Hot Now (20 slots) is NOT pure ranking. It's blended:

    Slots 1-10:  Top 10 by hotScore (organic winners)
    Slots 11-13: "Rising Fast" — rooms with largest hotScore delta (last 2h)
    Slots 14-16: "Mystery Rotation" — random rooms from ranks 20-100
                  (gives mid-tier a discoverability lifeline)
    Slots 17-18: "Editorial Pick" — 2 rooms hand-picked by ORBIT team daily
    Slots 19-20: "Fresh & Promising" — rooms <24h old, hotScore growing

  Users never know which slot is which. Feels organic.

  ── 10.5 · PER-USER PERSONALIZATION (Phase 2+) ────────────────────────────────

  For You tab uses Vertex AI or simple collaborative filtering:
    Signal inputs:
      - Rooms user has joined before (category preference)
      - Rooms user's close friends are in
      - Time-of-day activity patterns
      - Language preference detected from past messages
      - Regional proximity
    Output: 20 personalized rooms, refreshed every 15 min

  ── 10.6 · FIRESTORE SCHEMA ────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  rooms/{rid}/ranking:
    velocity, diversity, replyDepth, qualityAvg, retention, hotScore
    farmFlags: [string]
    farmRiskLevel: "clean" | "suspicious" | "farming" | "suspended"
    hotScoreDelta2h: number
    lastRankUpdateAt: timestamp

  discoveryFeed/hotNow:
    updatedAt: timestamp
    slots: [
      { rid, type: "organic"|"rising"|"mystery"|"editorial"|"fresh", rank: 1-20 }
    ]

    reason: "Women in Tech room doing amazing work this week"

  PRINCIPLE (Flow State — Csikszentmihalyi):
    Boredom = skill > challenge. Anxiety = challenge > skill.
    Flow = both matched. Detect drops, restore flow.

  SOLUTION: Passive detection + active intervention + graceful pause option.

  ── 13.1 · BOREDOM DETECTION ──────────────────────────────────────────────────

  Real-time signals (tracked client-side, synced every 60s):
    - Time since last interaction (tap, scroll, type)
    - Screen dwell time without action
    - Number of rooms opened then exited within 10s
    - Scroll depth decreasing session-over-session
    - Session duration trending down over 3 days

  Boredom triggers (in active session):
    T1: 3+ min in-app, no interaction → "Feeling quiet?" card appears
    T2: Opened+closed 3 rooms in <30s → "Looking for something new?" prompt
    T3: 5 min in same room, no post → "Prefer to listen? Voice room nearby"

  ── 13.2 · IN-SESSION INTERVENTIONS ───────────────────────────────────────────

    │  [👀 Just browse]        — curated feed   │
    │  [😴 Take a break]       — pause 1 hour   │
    └────────────────────────────────────────────┘

  Each option designed for different boredom types:
    - "Try new room": Discovery-curious
    - "Voice room": Low-effort alternative
    - "Reply to DM": Social connection
    - "Just browse": Passive consumption
    - "Take a break": Graceful exit (prevents churn!)

================================================================================

  ROOT CAUSE:
    Moderated, clean, polished app feels like an office. No mystery, no

    engagement is controversy-driven. We need controlled drama.

  PRINCIPLE (Optimal Arousal Theory):
    Humans need moderate stimulation. Too little = boredom.
    Too much = anxiety. Sweet spot = controlled unpredictability.

  SOLUTION: Weekly chaos events + controversy rooms + prediction markets.

  ── 15.1 · DEBATE ARENA (Weekly, Thursdays) ───────────────────────────────────

  Every Thursday, a hot topic auto-generated from trending rooms:
    Example: "Is Bollywood actually dying or just evolving?"

    - 48-hour debate period (Thu 8 PM → Sat 8 PM)
    - Users pick a side (tap "Yes" or "No")
    - Only sided users can post in the arena
    - Each side has its own sub-room
    - Audience can switch sides once (high cost: 100 💎)
    - Top-reacted argument from each side featured hourly
    - Final tally: side with more reactions wins
    - Winners get +50 💎, MVP argument gets 500 💎

  Creates: tribal engagement + stakes + social drama (safely contained)

  ── 15.2 · ROAST BATTLES (Saturday Nights, 9-11 PM) ───────────────────────────

    - Audience votes via reactions

    - Audience: earns 1 💎 per vote cast (engagement reward)

  Strict rules: no targeting ethnicity/religion/body. Auto-moderated by
  OpenAI Mod (fails → forfeit match).

  ── 15.3 · PREDICTION MARKETS ─────────────────────────────────────────────────

    "Will IPL 2026 Delhi Capitals reach top 4?"

    - Bet 10-100 💎 on Yes/No
    - 90% payout to winners (10% house fee)
    - Minimum 10 participants to resolve
    - Markets close 24h before event resolution
    - NO gambling addiction risk: max 100 💎 per bet, 500 💎 daily limit

  NOTE: Check Indian gaming law compliance (Public Gambling Act 1867 +
  state regulations). If risky, redesign as "Karma Predictions" —

================================================================================
  PART 16 — COMMUNITY MARSHAL PROGRAM
  (Fix for Problem 12: Founder Dependency)
================================================================================

  ROOT CAUSE:
    At 10K users, 500 reports/day. At 100K, 5000. Solo founder can't.
    Hired mods cost ₹30K/month each. Early stage can't afford.

  PRINCIPLE (Elinor Ostrom — Governing the Commons):
    Communities self-govern if given clear rules, tools, and accountability.
    Wikipedia, Reddit, StackOverflow — all moderator-powered.

  SOLUTION: 3-layer moderation — AI → Marshals → ORBIT Team.

  ── 16.1 · THE 3-LAYER FUNNEL ─────────────────────────────────────────────────

  Layer 1: AI Pre-Screen (Instant)
    - OpenAI Moderation API on every message (before display)
    - Hive AI on every image/video (before upload completion)
    - Auto-remove: clear violations (nudity, violence, hate)
    - Auto-flag: ambiguous cases → passes to Layer 2
    - Cost: ~₹0.01 per message, ₹0.05 per image

  Layer 2: Community Marshals (Hourly)
    - Flagged items enter a review queue
    - Marshals see 5 items at a time in a Tinder-style UI
    - Options: ✅ Keep / ⚠️ Warn / ❌ Remove / 🆘 Escalate
    - Requires 3 marshal agreements for action
    - Marshal earns 20 💎 per review
    - Average SLA: <1 hour for flagged content

  Layer 3: ORBIT Team (Daily)
    - Escalated by marshals
    - Account bans, legal issues, severe harassment
    - Human review by founder/team
    - SLA: <24 hours

  ── 16.2 · MARSHAL RECRUITMENT ────────────────────────────────────────────────

  Who qualifies:
    - User for ≥90 days
    - Account Health overall ≥85 for 30 days
    - Reputation score ≥90
    - No reports against them in 60 days
    - Passes a 20-question moderation quiz

  Application process:
    - User applies via Profile → "Become a Marshal"
    - Pass quiz (multi-choice, scenario-based)
    - 7-day trial period with shadow reviews (results don't count, mentor assesses)
    - Full marshal if trial passes

  ── 16.3 · MARSHAL COMPENSATION + STATUS ──────────────────────────────────────

  Earnings:
    - 20 💎 per reviewed item (avg 15-30/hour = 300-600 💎/hour)
    - 500 💎 weekly activity bonus (if >20 hours active)
    - 1,000 💎 monthly perfect-score bonus (>95% consensus agreement)

  Status:
    - Distinctive "🛡 Marshal" badge
    - Green border on profile
    - Separate marshal-only private room
    - Listed in "ORBIT Marshal Hall"
    - Priority customer support

  ── 16.4 · MARSHAL DASHBOARD ──────────────────────────────────────────────────

  Built as a Next.js route: /marshal (role-gated)

  Features:
    - Review queue (paginated, sorted by severity)
    - Statistics: items reviewed, consensus rate, earnings
    - Decision history with appeals
    - Marshal chat (discuss tough cases)
    - Training modules + monthly refreshers
    - Escalation button (to ORBIT team)
    - User history lookup (when reviewing)

  Simple UI, mobile-responsive, all Firestore-backed.

  ── 16.5 · APPEALS PROCESS ────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  If user disagrees with a decision:
    - 1-tap appeal from notification
    - Re-enters queue as "Appeal" (higher priority)
    - 3 DIFFERENT marshals review
    - If majority reverses: original decision overturned, marshal scores adjusted
    - If upheld: user gets detailed explanation

  Appeals reversal rate tracked per marshal (>30% reversal → retraining).

  ── 16.6 · QUARTERLY ELECTIONS ────────────────────────────────────────────────

  Marshals elected/re-elected every 3 months:
    - Community votes (all users eligible to vote)
    - Marshals can self-nominate
    - Top 100 voted in per quarter
    - Old marshals can re-run
    - Keeps accountability high + rotation possible

  ── 16.7 · FIRESTORE SCHEMA ────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  marshals/{uid}:
    status: "trial" | "active" | "suspended" | "retired"
    region: string
    languages: [string]
    joinedAt, trialEndsAt
    hoursThisWeek, hoursThisMonth
    reviewsCount, consensusAgreementRate
    appealsOverturnedAgainst: number
    creditsEarnedFromMod: number
    badges: [string]

    targetUid, contentId, contentType: "message" | "image" | "voice" | "room"

    severity: "low" | "med" | "high"

    consensusDecision: string
    status: "pending" | "reviewed" | "appealed" | "escalated"
    userAppealed: boolean
    finalDecision: string
    processedAt: timestamp

  marshalElections/{quarterId}:
    nominees: [{ uid, votes }]

================================================================================
  PART 36 — THE 10 NON-NEGOTIABLES
================================================================================

  Ye 10 rules kabhi bhi break nahi honge — regardless of pressure, scale,
  or competitor pressure. Ye ORBIT ki ATMA hain.

  ── 1. NEVER SHOW RUPEES IN-APP (except cashout screen) ───────────────────────

  Even if a designer pushes for it. Even if user asks. Never.
  Rupees = bank app. 💎 = game. We are a game.

  ── 2. NEVER SILENT-PENALIZE ─────────────────────────────────────────────────

  If earning drops, user must see why. Transparency > algorithmic efficiency.
  Account Health card is our social contract.

  ── 3. NEVER REWARD FAKE GROWTH ──────────────────────────────────────────────

  Squad rewards require 7-day activity + 50 msgs. No shortcuts.
  Vanity metrics destroy unit economics.

  ── 4. NEVER LET A ROOM BE A GHOST TOWN ──────────────────────────────────────

  AI Companions present (labeled) OR smart redirect to hot rooms.
  No empty screens. Ever.

  ── 5. NEVER SPAM NOTIFICATIONS ──────────────────────────────────────────────

  3/day hard cap. AI-curated. Emotional, not promotional.
  Each notification respects user's attention.

  ── 6. NEVER HIDE AI COMPANIONS ──────────────────────────────────────────────

  Every AI-generated profile, message, room seed — LABELED.
  DPDP Act compliance + user trust.

  ── 7. NEVER PERMABAN (except extreme) ───────────────────────────────────────

  Graduated penalties + redemption quests.
  Child safety + violence threats are only exceptions.

  ── 8. NEVER FORGET INDIA ────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  Hindi/Punjabi/Tamil support from Day 1.
  Festival rooms, cricket rooms, UPI, regional flavor.
  Silicon Valley patterns don't always work here.

  ── 9. NEVER SACRIFICE PRIVACY FOR ENGAGEMENT ────────────────────────────────

  No selling data. No targeted ads on sensitive content.
  Data stored in India. User's right to delete respected.

  ── 10. NEVER BUILD FOR IDEAL USERS ──────────────────────────────────────────

  Every feature tested against: fraudster, bored user, jealous user, lurker,
  emotional user. If it fails ANY, redesign.
  Real humans, not ideal users.
